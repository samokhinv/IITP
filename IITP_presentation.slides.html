2. Неразделимые классы¶

, .

c $\mathbf{w}, w_0, \mathbf{\xi}$, $C$: $$\underset{\mathbf{w}, w_0}{\min} \dfrac{1}{2}||\mathbf{w}||^2 + C\sum_{i=1}^N \xi_i,$$ $$s.t. \xi_i \geq 0,\; y_i(\mathbf{x}_i^T\mathbf{w} + w_0) \geq 1 - \xi_i, \; i = \overline{1,n}.$$

$$L = \dfrac{1}{2}||\mathbf{w}||^2 + C\sum_{i=1}^N\xi_i - \sum_{i=1}^N\lambda_i(y_i(\mathbf{x}_i\mathbf{w} + w_0) - (1-\xi_i)) - \sum_{i=1}^{N}\mu_i\xi_i$$

,

$$\mathbf{w} = \sum_{i=1}^{N}\lambda_i y_i \mathbf{x}_i$$ $$0 = \sum_{i=1}^{N}\lambda_i y_i$$ $$\lambda_i = C - \mu_i$$

: $$L = \sum_{i=1}^{N}\lambda_i - \dfrac{1}{2}\sum_{i=1}^N\sum_{k=1}^N\lambda_i\lambda_ky_iy_k\mathbf{x}_i^T\mathbf{x} _k$$ -:
